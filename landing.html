About Me

My name is Bongiwe Sandi Nandi Mkwananzi, but you can call me Bongi. I’m an international student from Zimbabwe and a junior studying Computer Science with a minor in International Studies at Bethune-Cookman University. As a first-generation college student, I’m navigating this exciting journey with a keen interest in AI, Robotics, and Machine Learning. I am mostly self-taught in programming and continually enhance my skills through various courses.

What I Enjoy

I have a variety of interests that keep me busy. I love watching anime, crocheting, sketching, and hair braiding. These hobbies help me relax and express my creativity. Also, my name is pronounced as "Bong-ee-way," so now you can say it just right!

My Involvement

I’m actively involved in several campus organizations. I serve as the Vice President and Hardware Team Lead of the Robotics Club, Treasurer of the NSBE BCU chapter, and President of the African Students Association, where I also choreograph and teach dance. These roles provide me with hands-on experience and a chance to apply the skills I’ve gained.

Let’s Connect!

I’m enthusiastic about meeting new people and making meaningful connections. Whether you’re interested in discussing technology, sharing hobbies, or just having a friendly chat, I’d love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out and let’s connect!
